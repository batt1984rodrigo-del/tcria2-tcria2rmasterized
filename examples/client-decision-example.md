# Example Client Decision Recommendation

> Fictional demonstration for product evaluation. It is not legal advice or a real institutional decision.

## Review question

Should Supplier Aurora Ltd. be approved based exclusively on the submitted qualification document batch?

## Recommended decision

# PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS

The submitted batch supports preliminary supplier qualification. However, final approval should remain conditional because one required certificate is expired and one ownership declaration was not found.

## Why this decision is recommended

### Evidence supporting continuation

- Company registration was found and is readable.
- The registered company name is consistent across the reviewed documents.
- The insurance certificate is valid at the review date.
- Technical qualification evidence was found for the requested service category.
- The integrity declaration is signed and dated.

### Conditions preventing unconditional approval

- The tax clearance certificate is expired.
- The beneficial ownership declaration was not found in the submitted batch.
- One scanned page has low OCR confidence and requires visual confirmation.

## Required next actions

1. Request a current tax clearance certificate.
2. Request the beneficial ownership declaration.
3. Visually confirm the low-confidence scanned page.
4. Re-run or update the review after the missing evidence is received.
5. Submit the resulting report to the responsible compliance or management authority for final approval.

## Evidence table

| Finding | Evidence reference | Status | Effect on recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Company registration confirmed | `DOC-001`, page 1 | Confirmed | Supports continuation |
| Insurance certificate valid | `DOC-004`, page 2 | Confirmed | Supports continuation |
| Tax clearance expired | `DOC-006`, page 1 | Unresolved | Requires updated document |
| Ownership declaration absent | Batch inventory | Missing | Prevents unconditional approval |
| Low OCR confidence | `DOC-008`, page 3 | Human review | Requires visual confirmation |

## Limits

- The result applies only to the submitted batch.
- TCRIA does not confirm that the client submitted every relevant document.
- No external registry was consulted in this fictional example.
- The recommendation may change when updated evidence is submitted.
- The final decision remains with the client's authorized professional or institution.

## Client-facing conclusion

The program does not merely say “approve” or “reject.” It shows the recommended course of action, explains the evidence behind it, identifies the conditions that must be resolved and preserves the final decision for the responsible human authority.
